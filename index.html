Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)




South Meadows Recovery - Austin IOP Drug & Alcohol Rehab

Our behavioral health team may recommend our Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP) as the best plan of care for your addiction concerns based on the results or your initial consultation and assessment. PHP can also be considered a great transition from an inpatient facility setting. The core of PHP is evidence-based psychotherapy in a small group format. Our licensed medical providers oversee medication management when necessary. Our PHP program includes holistic activities in programming to encourage relaxation and mindfulness. Some of these activities include art groups and meditation classes. In our PHP program, our clients will have 5 hours of programming, 5 days each week.
